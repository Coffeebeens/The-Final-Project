The process of adopting a cat:

1. Initial expression of interest:
First of all, you need to fill in an expression of interest to show your
interest in adopting a cat. This can be done online, via an application form or by visiting the cat
cafe in person. In the registration of interest, you may be asked to enter information about yourself,
your lifestyle, your previous experiences with cats and what you are looking for in a feline companion.

2. Evaluation and matching:
After you have submitted your expression of interest, the staff at the cat
cafe will evaluate it and assess whether you are a suitable cat owner. They may contact you to ask br additional questions or to schedule a personal interview or visit.

Based on your answers and preferences, they will then try to match you with a suitable cat. They take into
account factors such as the cat's personality, energy level, any special needs and how well they
think the cat would fit into your home and lifestyle.

3. Personal Meeting and Evaluation:
If you have been matched with a cat, the next step will usually be
a personal meeting. You get the opportunity to meet and interact with the cat to see if there is a
mutual connection and if the cat seems like a good fit for you and your family.

The staff can also conduct a home inspection to make sure your home is safe and suitable for a cat.
This may mean they check that you have the necessary precautions such as cat-friendly furniture, a safe
outdoor environment if applicable and no hazardous substances or areas that could harm the cat.
4. Approval and Adoption Agreement:
If all goes well during the meeting and possible home inspection,
you can be approved as an adoptive owner. You will then be asked to sign an adoption agreement and
pay any adoption fee. The adoption agreement is a legal document that sets out the terms of
the adoption and your responsibilities as a cat owner.

The adoption agreement may include details about providing the cat with veterinary care, raising it in
a healthy manner, not abandoning it, and returning it to the cat cafe if you can no longer care for it.

Cafe Rules safety guidelines


  • ♡ We accept cash, credit card, and various forms of digital currency.

  • ♡ Age limit: 6+
    Those between the ages of 6-12 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

  • ♡ Please refrain from bringing any food or beverage inside the premise
    We offer drink incase you're thirsty.

  • ♡ All of our cafes have a maximum charge system in place, so please feel free to make use of it.
    The maximum charge is applied after 2 hours, so you can take your time without feeling rushed.

  • ♡ Our cafes have Wi-Fi, power supplies, and a variety of chargers.
    Please make use of them at your leisure.

  • ♡ It can be relatively crowded on holidays between 2:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.
    Please be aware that depending on the level of congestion inside you may be asked to wait before you are permitted to
    enter.

  • ♡ Our establishments are not barrier-free, so those that require a wheelchair will be declined entry.
    Please either have a companion escort you inside, or make use of a walking stick.
    We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in ensuring the safety of our customers.

  • ♡ Please refrain from bringing treats and toys for the cats.
    Treats and toys are already provided inside the cafe. Please feel free to make use of them.
    Cat treats are sold only between the hours of 11 a.m. - 7:00 p.m.
    Quantities are limited, so please ask a member of staff if you would like some.

  • ♡ You can take photos and videos, but please refrain from using the flash function.
    You can post them on social media and personal blogs, but please refrain from using them for commercial purposes.
    You can film YouTube videos too, but please be considerate of other customers while you are filming.
